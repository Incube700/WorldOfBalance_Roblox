# Playtest v0.2 Test Script

## Fresh Studio Play

1. Start Play from a clean Studio session.
2. Confirm the player spawns in the lobby with a controllable tank.
3. Confirm armor zones are visible and synced to the hull.
4. Shoot once and confirm the world reload bar fills back up.

## Duel

1. Enter DuelPad with two players.
2. Confirm old HP/reload panels stay hidden.
3. Play to a final match win.
4. Confirm Duel winner receives the configured Crystal reward.
5. Confirm no BattleArena upgrades or cosmetic state changes Duel damage, health, reload, or movement.

## Armor Readability

1. Shoot front armor and confirm `NO PEN` or damage is understandable.
2. Shoot at an angle and confirm `RICOCHET` feedback appears without damage flash.
3. Shoot side/rear and confirm penetration damage feedback appears.
4. Confirm feedback is short-lived and does not spam the screen.

## BattleArena

1. Drive into ArenaPad.
2. Confirm Arena HUD says to fight bots and earn Bolts.
3. Confirm solo arena maintains bots up to the configured easy count.
4. Let bots shoot the player and confirm player can die/respawn.
5. Kill a bot and confirm score, kills, and Bolts increase.
6. Kill a bot with a ricochet if possible and confirm bonus score/Bolts.
7. Confirm bot death and respawn works.
8. Return to Lobby and confirm bots clean up when no players remain.

## Mobile

1. Join from the published link on a phone.
2. Confirm MOVE, AIM, and FIRE remain usable.
3. Confirm Arena stats are compact and do not cover controls.
4. Confirm Duel score remains readable.
5. Confirm feedback text is readable without becoming huge.

## Tester Questions

- Did you understand what to do?
- Did ricochet feel fun?
- Did armor angle make sense?
- Did mobile controls feel okay?
- Did you want another round?
